Our History

Wine is an alcoholic beverage made from grapes, generally
Vitis vinifera, fermented without the addition of sugars, acids,
enzymes, water, or other nutrients. Yeast consumes the sugar in
the grapes and converts it to ethanol and carbon dioxide.
Different varieties of grapes and strains of yeasts
produce different styles of wine.
